A Guide to Time Travel for Fun or Business

This manual contains everything that you need to successfully time travel.

Part 1 - Avoiding Paradoxes

In this section, you learn how to avoid paradoxes. Some things to avoid:

  • Killing your grandfather.
  • Convincing your younger self not to time travel.
  • Stepping on butterflies.

Part 2 - Top 3 Reasons to Consider a Time Travel Vacation

Here are the top three reasons (in order) to time travel during your next vacation:

  1. You can return immediately after you leave.
  2. You can finally find all of those items your younger self lost.
  3. You can see Star Wars episode 99 today.
